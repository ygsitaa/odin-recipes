Lasagna

Sweet Lasagna with Bolognese Sauce and pork.

Ingredients:
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb ground pork
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Steps:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook ground beef and pork over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  5. Layer 4 lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  6. Add half of the meat mixture, half of the ricotta mixture, and a third of the mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat layers, ending with noodles topped with marinara sauce and remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly.
  9. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving.
